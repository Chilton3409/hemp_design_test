About us

Named after one of the oldest settlements founded in 1722 and created on a late 1800's farm with one feeling in our hearts to create a better sustainable medicine for our families and our animals.

"Let food be thy medicine and medicine be thy food."
Hippocrates

My Dedication

I gained the dedication to acheive this as a young girl learning regenerative growing practices from my grandfather. I started a small hog farm at the age fo 19 and never looked back. Over the last 20 years, I have spent my life and expressed my love for alpacas by saving a rare almost lost breed originally bred for their color variations and beauty.

Legalization of hemp

Keeping my eye for beauty and the long-awaited legalization of hemp through the passing of the 2018 farm bill I dove headfirst into making our dream a reality. Learning how to farm hemp and creating the lotion that is our flagship product. It takes a community to make it happen on our farm, your family, and of course the alpacas. Together we can bring the heritage of the land and family to create products and heal both while achieving 100% sustainable practices coming as close to 0 inputs as possible. Healing our earth and bodies with the beauty of nature and the art of hemp
